Home Sales Ready Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026. This policy explains what personal information we collect when you use Home Sales Ready, why we collect it, and what we do with it. We have written it in plain English and kept legal jargon to a minimum. If anything is unclear, please get in touch.

1. Who we are

Home Sales Ready is a digital platform that helps property owners in England and Wales organise and share the documents and information needed to sell their home. The service is available at www.homesalesready.com.

For the purposes of data protection law, we are the data controller — meaning we decide how and why your personal information is used.

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at: hello@kadimasystems.co.uk

2. What information we collect

We collect information in two ways: information you give us directly, and information collected automatically when you use the site.

Information you provide

  • Your name and email address when you create an account
  • Your Google account details if you choose to sign in using Google
  • Property information and documents you upload to your data room
  • Any messages you send to us via email or a contact form

Information collected automatically

  • Your IP address and browser type
  • Pages you visit on our site and how long you spend on them
  • Technical information about the device you use to access the site
We do not collect any special category data (such as health information, financial account details, or information about ethnicity or religion) as part of our core service.

3. Why we collect your information and the legal basis for doing so

Under UK data protection law, we must have a valid reason — known as a "lawful basis" — for collecting and using your information. Here is what we collect and why:

  • To provide the service: We use your account details and uploaded documents to run your data room. Without this, we simply cannot provide the platform. (Lawful basis: performance of a contract.)
  • To keep your account secure: We use login information to verify who you are and protect your data. (Lawful basis: legitimate interests.)
  • To improve the platform: We use anonymised usage data to understand how people use Home Sales Ready and make it better. (Lawful basis: legitimate interests.)
  • To send you service updates: If something important changes about your account or the platform, we will email you. (Lawful basis: legitimate interests or contract, depending on the nature of the update.)
  • To comply with the law: In some cases we may be required to retain or share information to meet a legal obligation. (Lawful basis: legal obligation.)

4. Signing in with Google

We offer the option to create and access your account using your Google account. If you choose this route, Google will share your name and email address with us so we can set up your account. We do not receive your Google password, and we do not access any other part of your Google account.

Google's own privacy policy governs how they handle your information on their end. You can read it at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. How long we keep your information

We keep your account information and uploaded documents for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, unless we are required to keep it for longer by law (for example, for tax or legal compliance purposes).

Anonymised usage statistics may be kept indefinitely as they cannot be linked back to you.

6. Who we share your information with

We do not sell your personal information to anyone. We may share it in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We work with a small number of trusted third-party companies (for example, cloud hosting providers) who process data on our behalf. They are only permitted to use your data to provide services to us, and are bound by data protection agreements.
  • Legal requirements: If we are required by law, court order, or a regulatory authority to share information, we will do so.
  • Business transfers: If Home Sales Ready is acquired by or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that process. We would notify you beforehand.

7. Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the following rights in relation to your personal information:

  • Access: You can ask us for a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can ask us to correct any information that is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Deletion: You can ask us to delete your information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Restriction: You can ask us to limit how we use your information while a dispute is resolved.
  • Portability: You can ask us to send your information to you or a third party in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: You can object to us using your information where we rely on legitimate interests as our lawful basis.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at hello@kadimasystems.co.uk. We will respond within one month.

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your information, you also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK's data protection regulator, at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookies

We use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to keep you signed in and to understand how the site is being used. We do not use cookies to serve you adverts.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Please be aware that disabling certain cookies may affect how the site works, including keeping you signed in.

9. How we keep your information safe

We take the security of your information seriously. Access to your data room is protected by your account login, and all data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). We restrict internal access to personal data to only those who need it to operate the service.

No online platform can guarantee complete security, but we follow industry best practices and review our security arrangements regularly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time — for example, when we add new features or if the law changes. When we make significant changes, we will let you know by email or by a notice on the site. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
